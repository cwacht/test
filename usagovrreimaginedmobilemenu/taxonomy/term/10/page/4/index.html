Lost or Stolen Passports

  • Read more about Lost or Stolen Passports

How to Report Your Lost or Stolen Passport

  • Online: Use the wizard to submit Form DS-64 online.
  • By phone: Call toll-free 1-877-487-2778 (TTY 1-888-874-7793).
  • By mail: Use Form DS-64 and mail it to the address on the form.

Once you have reported your passport lost or stolen, the State Department invalidates it. You cannot use it if you find it.

Children Under 16

All children under 16 must appear in person to apply. A parent (preferably both) must be present and must sign the passport application.

How to Get Money if You Are in a Foreign Country During an Emergency

  • Read more about How to Get Money if You Are in a Foreign Country During an Emergency

If you are outside the U.S. and need money in an emergency, there are a number of ways you can have money sent from a friend or family member. These include:

  • Bank transfer
  • Credit cards
  • Wire transfer

If these options are not possible, the State Department maybe be able to help your friend or family member transfer the money. 

Learn how to get emergency financial assistance abroad.

How to Get Emergency Assistance if You Are in a Foreign Country

  • Read more about How to Get Emergency Assistance if You Are in a Foreign Country

If you are a U.S. citizen who needs assistance outside the U.S., find and contact the nearest U.S. embassy for help with:

  • Foreign crises and natural disasters
  • Lost or stolen passports
  • Medical emergencies
  • Crime or arrest
  • Missing persons
  • Parental child abductions
  • Death abroad

If you are concerned about a family member outside the U.S., call 1-888-407-4747.

Visas for U.S. Citizens Traveling Abroad

  • Read more about Visas for U.S. Citizens Traveling Abroad

While not all countries require visas for American travelers, many do. Look up your destination using the Department of State's Learn About Your Destination search tool. On the country’s information page, you will find entry, exit, and visa requirements. You will also find travel advisories and a link to the country’s embassy.
