Welfare Benefits or Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)

  • Read more about Welfare Benefits or Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)

Recipients may qualify for help with:

  • Food
  • Housing
  • Home energy
  • Child care
  • Job training

Each state or tribal government runs its TANF program differently and has a different name.

Get Food Assistance With the WIC Program

  • Read more about Get Food Assistance With the WIC Program

Find Out If You Are Eligible for WIC and Apply

  • If you are applying for yourself, you must be at least one of the following:
    • Pregnant
    • Breastfeeding (up to the baby's first birthday)
    • Within six months of having given birth or pregnancy ending
  • If you are applying for your children, they must be under 5 years old.
  • You must meet other WIC eligibility requirements 

Obtenga asistencia alimentaria con el programa WIC

  • Read more about Obtenga asistencia alimentaria con el programa WIC

Averigüe si es elegible para recibir beneficios del WIC

  • Si solicita el programa para usted, debe encontrarse en una de las siguientes situaciones:
    • estar embarazada,
    • estar amamantando,
    • estar dentro de los seis primeros meses después de haber dado a luz o de haber finalizado el embarazo.
  • Si solicita el programa para sus hijos, deben ser menores de cinco años.
  • Debe cumplir con otros
