Voter Registration

  • Read more about Voter Registration

Voter Registration Deadlines

  • Read more about Voter Registration Deadlines

Every state except North Dakota requires citizens to register if they want to become voters. Depending on your state, the registration deadline could be as much as a month before an election.

Check the U.S. Vote Foundation to find your state's voter registration deadline. You can also check your state or territory's election office for more details.

Update or Change Your Voter Registration

  • Read more about Update or Change Your Voter Registration

You will need to update or change your voter registration if you:

  • Move within your state
  • Change your name
  • Want to change your political party affiliation

If you move permanently to another state, register to vote in that new state.

Find your state’s registration deadline, and submit your changes before it. That could be up to 30 days before the election.

  • Read more about How to Get a Voter Registration Card
